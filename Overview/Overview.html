The IBM 1620 Project Team

This site was created, and is maintained, by the IBM 1620 Project Team at the Computer History Museum in Mountain View, California, USA.

This team was formed in the Fall of 1998 with the initial goals of restoring the museum's IBM 1620 Model 1 computer to operation and collecting as much information and material on the IBM 1620 / 1710 / 1720 as possible. The project was successful and is documented here.

A subset of the team started a new project in 2016 to build a functional replica of the IBM 1620, called the IBM 1620 Jr., that is rugged enough to stand up to hands-on use by museum visitors. That project is documented here.
