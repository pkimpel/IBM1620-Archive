Welcome to the IBM 1620 website, the largest repository of IBM 1620 information in the world.

The IBM 1620 was IBM's low-end, scientific computer in the early 1960s.

This site was created, and is maintained, by the IBM 1620 Project Team at the Computer History Museum in Mountain View, California, USA.

This team was formed in the Fall of 1998 with the initial goals of restoring the museum's IBM 1620 Model 1 to operation and collecting as much information and material on the IBM 1620 / 1710 / 1720 as possible. The project was successful and is documented here. [put link to Restoration page here]

A subset of the team started a new project in 2016 to build a functional replica of the IBM 1620, called the IBM 1620 Jr., that is rugged enough to stand up to hands-on use by museum visitors. That project is documented here. [put link to IBM 1620 Jr page here]
