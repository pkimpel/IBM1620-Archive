IBM 1620 Documents

During the IBM 1620's lifetime, many documents were produced by IBM and others. These includes documentation about the machine itself, how to program & operate it, and how it was used.

IBM's own archive only contains a few manuals, press releases, and photos for the IBM 1620. All of the other documents on this website were collected over 25 years by the Computer History Museum's IBM 1620 Team.

These documents are organized into these categories:

Subsection Description
Ads Ads for or related to the IBM 1620 published by IBM and other companies.
Articles Published articles about the IBM 1620 and how it was used.
Books Published books exclusively about the IBM 1620 or which have chapters/sections about it.
Manuals IBM published manuals for the IBM 1620 and closely related topics.
Miscellaneous Other documentation which doesn't fit into the other categories.
Papers and Reports Published papers, reports, technical notes, and theses about the IBM 1620.
Patents Patents granted to IBM for the IBM 1620.
Press Releases Press releases about the IBM 1620 and related topics from IBM and other companies.
Specifications Formal specifications of the IBM 1620.
User Group Meetings Announcements, agenda, and proceedings for the IBM 1620 User Group and COMMON meetings.
